Caesar demands that the Germans leave Gaul, but they say that they
cannot endure the Suebi. He then proposes that they settle among the
Ubii, just on the other side of the Rhine. The Germans ask that Caesar
remain where he is while this proposal is being considered, but Caesar
thinks this is a ruse to gain time.
